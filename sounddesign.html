John Lemon - 3D Stealth Game: Haunted House

This is the first project I did after finishing my FMOD course. All the audio you hear in this video was recorded, edited and implemented by me using Ableton Live, FMOD and Unity.
John Lemon is a free Unity 3D learn asset available to download for free on their asset store.

First Person Shooter (Low Poly)

This is a free unity project by INFIMA games which I adapted with audio. From the music composition to recording folley and usisng pre-recorded assets, editing, mixing and implementing using FMOD.
The Unity project is free to download here on the Unity AssetStore.

Viking Village

This is the result of my learning course for FMOD and Unity. I recorded and edited my own sound effects and put together the background music in a few hours. It was a great learning project! This is a gameplay footage of the end result.
Viking Village is a free Unity 3D learning asset available to download on the Unity AssetStore.

Orbit

Orbit is a mobile game released back in 2020 on the Google Play store. I was in charge of the entire audio production including sound effects, UI, and music. Unfortunately, the game is no longer available, but I still have this game play footage outlining my work.

Topoly

Topoly is a low poly endless run, fast paced indie game made by Quadratic Games available for Windows, Linux, and Android. I did the sound design and ambient audio for the game as part of my portfolio work back in 2020. Topoly is available to buy online and you can find me in the credits under my alias Idem Grey. Find the game here: Topoly by Quadratic Games
