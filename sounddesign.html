About me

My name is Kaloyan and I am an audio creative. I'm based in Amsterdam, and I promote all my content under the alias Idem Grey.

Ever since I first touched a soundboard I have been obsessed with all things audio - live audio engineering, multigenre music production, sound design for games and films, post production for podcasts. If it makes a sound, I want to be a part of it!

Skills & Tools

I am proficient with Ableton Live as my heart of operation, and I have a solid collection of industry standard plugins under my tool belt with over five years of experience.

For sound design I use a combination of field recordings, synthesis and foley techniques to create immersive soundscapes and sound effects.

My studio is equipped for multi-track indoor and outdoor recording. I have hands on experience with FMOD Studio and Unity, providing sound design assets and audio implementation remotely with Unity Version Control and Git. I can also easily adapt any video material with music or environmental audio using DaVinci Resolve.

For email inquiries you can reach me on idemgrey@gmail.com.
