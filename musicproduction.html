Luther McGinnis - Take Care Of Me (2025)

In this project, I had the opportunity to apply my expertise in the R&B/Neo-Soul genre and re-produce an existing song for my client Luther McGinnis. I essentially had to rearrange the song structure together with Luther. Sticking to the original key progression I adapted my sound to his harmonics idea with the direction to deliver a more modern sound but with a touch of my creativity. You can find me in the credits section under my alias Idem Grey.
