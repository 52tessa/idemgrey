My Services

I offer different services depending on the needs of your project. This page highlights the most common ones.

Original music productions

As a multi-genre music producer I offer production services for a range of artists, taking care of song arrangement and composition. I am most experienced with hiphop, RnB, drum and bass and pop music, but am always keen to take on a new challenge regardless of the genre. Whether you have a set idea already or are looking to get a fresh beat, I am up for the task at hand.

Mixing & Mastering

I offer pre- and post-production services, both on my own productions as well as on near-finished songs.

(Vocal) recording

My studio is set up for simple vocal recording. However I can also edit vocal recordings in post and apply vocal tuning where necessary and wanted. Aside from vocal recording, the studio is also equipped to record instruments like guitar or keyboard. I am also geared up for outdoor recording, whether it is vocals or ambience.

FX & UI sound design

Field and foley recording

Voiceovers for animations and games

Soundtracks, background music and ambience
