About me

My name is Kaloyan and I am an audio creative delivering music production, sound design and audio engineering for games, multi-media projects and performing artists. I promote all my music content under the alias Idem Grey.

Skills & Tools

I am proficient with Ableton Live as my heart of operation, with over five years of experience and a solid collection of industry standard plugins under my tool belt such as Waves, iZotope, FabFilter, Tone2, Native Instruments and many more.

For sound design I use a combination of field recordings, synthesis and foley techniques to create immersive soundscapes and sound effects.

My studio is equipped for multi-track indoor and outdoor recording. I have hands on experience with FMOD Studio and Unity, providing sound design assets and audio implementation remotely with Unity Version Control and Git. I can also easily adapt any video material with music or environmental audio using DaVinci Resolve.
