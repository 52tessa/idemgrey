About me

My name is Kaloyan and I am a multi-genre music producer & sound designer. I'm based in Amsterdam, and I promote all my content under the alias Idem Grey. You can find all my work on my website.

Conventional music & multimedia sound design production

I work with performing artists and like-minded producers, offering the entire song production cycle - pre-production, recording, arrangement, vocal arrangement, post-edit, mixing & mastering to release. Apart from my focus in conventional music production, I also do audio adaptations for videos, games and films. That includes sound effects, environmental and foley audio, voice overs, animations or cinematic and game play adaptive music.

Skills & Tools

My studio is equipped for multi-track and outdoor recording. I am proficient with Ableton Live as my heart of operation, and I have a solid collection of industry standard plugins under my tool belt with over five years of experience. Apart from that I have recently finished my FMOD/Unity course for game audio implementation, but I can also easily adapt any video material with music or environmental audio.
